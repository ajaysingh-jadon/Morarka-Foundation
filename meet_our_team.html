Ms. Pragati Mundhra

Ms. Pragati Raghavdas Mundhra serves as a Director at M. R. Morarka GDC Rural Research Foundation, where she brings strategic direction to various rural development and sustainability initiatives. In addition to this role, she holds directorships in leading organizations including Gannon Dunkerley and Co. Ltd., Datson Exports Ltd., and Greenearth Organic Products Pvt. Ltd., among others. Her leadership spans industries such as engineering, organic agriculture, and microfinance, reflecting a strong commitment to both industrial excellence and social responsibility.

Taking forward the legacy of her father, Shri Kamal Morarka, she actively supports philanthropic efforts in Nawalgarh, including the development of Belabai Vidya Mandir School, and promotes community awareness and education through school outreach, pre-school education, wholesome nutrition, health, hygiene, and women empowerment and she works passionately toward realizing his vision of sustainable progress in their ancestral village and surrounding areas. Rooted in the values instilled by her father—truthfulness, resilience, and courage—she continues to uphold the principle that challenges must be faced head-on, a philosophy deeply embedded in the Morarka family ethos and one that continues to guide her path.

Guided by focused initiatives in vermicomposting, organic farming, skill development, workplace safety, community welfare, and workforce diversity, her leadership is steering the organization toward a transformative path, seamlessly integrating Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles into its core strategy and operations.

Mr. Madan Singh Shekhawat

Mr. Madan Singh Shekhawat brings with him over four decades of rich experience in the field of finance and administrative experience. He served as the Head of the Finance Department at Gannon Dunkerley & Co. Ltd. (GDCL), from 1977 to 2019, where he played a pivotal role in steering the company's financial operations. Currently, he holds the position of Chief Executive Officer at both the M.R. Morarka GDC Rural Research Foundation, Rajasthan and Morarka Organic Food Limited, Jaipur. With extensive expertise in accounts and finance, Mr. Shekhawat continues to contribute strategically to the growth and governance of these organizations

Ms. Deepal Solanki

Ms. Deepal Solanki is a dedicated Rural Development and Child Rights professional with over 14 years of experience in the development sector, specializing in child protection, governance systems, and inclusive rural development. She holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Rural Management from IIHMR University, Jaipur, and a PG Diploma in Environment and Sustainable Development from IGNOU. Currently associated with Morarka Organic as Assistant General Manager – Projects, she previously served in impactful roles with Child Rights & You (CRY), Antakshari Foundation, HCM RIPA, and Gayatri Seva Sansthan, leading initiatives supported by UNICEF and other national bodies.

Ms. Solanki’s core expertise lies in policy advocacy, programme development, stakeholder engagement, research, training, and documentation—particularly in the implementation of child protection laws like the JJ Act, POCSO, and ICPS. Her work has involved strengthening child-friendly governance structures across Rajasthan and Gujarat, with a strong focus on tribal-dominated regions. She has contributed articles to The Times of India and co-authored resources on gender issues and child rights. With extensive experience in partnership management, grant handling, and capacity building, she continues to drive impactful, community-focused development interventions.
