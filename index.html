Morarka Foundation

A not for profit voluntary organization was set up by Mr. Kamal M. Morarka in 1993 to create wealth for the nation through innovations in resource management and capacity building. Efforts made by team, it has become possible so that from being a successful grass-root level implementing agency for 28 years, today it is also being recognized as a leading resource organisation offering solutions for sustainable agriculture in the world.

Human Resources

In its history of 20 Years, Morarka Foundation has been growing in leaps and bounds. From just a small team of 5 persons in 1993-94, today it has become a 500+ strong team spread all over India. Outside India with strategic collaborations, it has many more professionals on board. The workforce is divided according to the functional /intervention areas.In many places where it has collaborative initiatives the workforce is generally engaged by the partners.

Major Infrastructure Milestone

The year 2006 has been especially important and can be considered as a landmark year for resource development.
