Project Overview

Morarka Foundation, in partnership with the Rajasthan State AIDS Control Society (RSACS), has been implementing a Targeted Intervention (TI) Project for Female Sex Workers (FSWs) in Jaisalmer since August 2006. The initiative focuses on HIV/AIDS awareness, prevention, and access to essential health services through outreach, IEC activities, workshops, and free condom distribution.

The project aims to reduce new infections, increase STI treatment uptake, ensure consistent and correct condom use, and strengthen linkages with existing care and support services. By building community rapport, mobilizing participation, training peer educators, and improving service delivery systems, the programme works toward creating an informed, empowered, and health-seeking community while ensuring effective monitoring, management, and quality of intervention.
