Project Overview

The Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in 200 villages of Gwalior aims to ensure safe and reliable drinking water for every rural household through the provision of Functional Household Tap Connections (FHTCs). Under this initiative, comprehensive water supply systems are being developed and strengthened to deliver clean, potable water in a sustainable and efficient manner.

The project focuses on improving water infrastructure across multiple villages in the Gwalior district, including the construction of new pipelines, overhead tanks, distribution networks, and source augmentation systems. Special emphasis is placed on water quality monitoring, community participation, and long-term operation and maintenance.

Our involvement in the Jal Jeevan Mission Gwalior Project reflects our commitment to supporting government efforts in enhancing public health, reducing water-borne diseases, and improving the quality of life for rural communities. Through collaborative planning, on-ground execution, and continuous monitoring.
