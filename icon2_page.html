Product Portfolio - Technology & Process Planning

Research & Development

Morarka Foundation has invested over 70% of its own resources in R&D, becoming a leading non-governmental resource organization in the country. Most innovations are in cutting-edge agricultural technologies.

Organic Cultivation Technology

Field-based trials with farmers helped test and implement new organic techniques immediately. Practices have been developed for 180+ crops in over 10 agro-climatic zones across India.

Biotechnology for Agriculture

Morarka's biotech lab contributed breakthroughs like enriched vermiculture, probiotics for pest management, and scientific waste management. Their lab also ensures strict quality control of organic food items.

Storage, Processing & Value Addition

Morarka pioneered fumigation-free storage, solar-based dehydration, and custom equipment for processing. Over 30 technologies have been developed in just three years.

Information Technology Applications

IT tools are used to manage farm data, certification records, and product traceability. Custom software allows tracking of thousands of farmers according to international certification standards.

Procurement, Storage, Processing & Value Addition

Operations are managed by local entrepreneurs and farmers. Processing centers handle wheat, rice, pulses, frozen foods, and more with in-house quality control in a 35,000 sq.ft. facility.

Marketing, Distribution & Retail Infrastructure

Retail and bulk sales are conducted from Jaipur, Delhi, and Mumbai. Products are sold through direct delivery, modern retail, and Morarka's own stores.

Organic Cultivation: Technology & Practices

  • Participatory approach involving local entrepreneurs and farmers.
  • 40% reduction in cultivation costs in many cases.
