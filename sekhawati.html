About the Festival

Since 1994, Morarka Foundation has been organizing the Shekhawati Festival in partnership with the State Tourism Department and District Administration, turning it into one of the region’s most celebrated rural tourism events. Held at Surya Mandal Stadium, the festival showcases Shekhawati’s rich culture through traditional performances, local games, craft bazaars, art competitions, and community recognition awards.

A major highlight is the Organic Food Court—featuring over 50 stalls serving exclusively organic dishes and offering visitors an authentic farm-to-plate experience. The festival continues to draw tourists from across India and abroad, celebrating the heritage, talent, and vibrant spirit of Shekhawati.
