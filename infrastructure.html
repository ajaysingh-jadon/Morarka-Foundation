Morarka Foundation began with a 1000 sq. ft. office in Nawalgarh and a 300 sq. ft. space in Jaipur, with a small team of 10. Within three years, it expanded to a 4000 sq. ft. office and one acre of leased land in Nawalgarh, and a 500 sq. ft. office in Jaipur, with a team of 40 field professionals. By five years, it had its own office and Research Centre in Nawalgarh, a 5000 sq. ft. government-provided office in Jaipur, access to 10 acres of land, and a team of 100 professionals.

Major Infrastructure Milestone

Morarka Foundation expanded significantly, establishing branch and field offices across India and one in Malaysia. By 2006, a major milestone was achieved with the construction of a fully furnished 20,000 sq. ft. office in Jaipur, featuring a conference hall, library, biotech lab, classrooms, stores, and a model retail cum restaurant. The facility accommodates around 70 middle and senior-level professionals.
