Our Approach

At M.R Morarka GDC Rural Research Foundation we take responsibility for using our resources efficiently, achieving results and being accountable to our supporters, partners and most of all, Women and the under-privileged sections of the society. We value new innovation and ideas, the innovations in the field excite and gave a ray of hope in doing concrete with the sustainable development for the stakeholders and beneficiaries. And we believe in constantly working up with creative and novel solutions for the betterment of weaker sections of the society.

We believe that the quality of life in remote and deprived areas is majorly dependent on the livelihood prospects in the area, also depends majorly on the literacy level of the women’s and children of the community, the effective and optimal use of natural resources offered by the geography & the capacity building of human resource and institutions. Developmental efforts in any one of these areas complement growth in the other two and trigger a cycle of sustainability thereby promoting the holistic development of the community. Hence, M.R Morarka GDC Rural Research Foundation ensures that each of its interventions further towards ‘Sustainable Integrated Development’
