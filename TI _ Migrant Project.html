Project Overview

Since 2014, M.R. Morarka Foundation has been implementing a migrant-focused HIV/AIDS intervention in Barmer, a district with high in-migration and out-migration from states such as Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Odisha, Bihar, and West Bengal.

The project aims to deliver effective behaviour-change communication, provide free STI checkups to 10,000 migrants, promote consistent condom use, offer care and support to HIV-positive individuals, and create an environment that strengthens service delivery and community well-being.
