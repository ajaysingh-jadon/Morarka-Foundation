Human Resource Policy and Recruitment

Morarka Foundation has grown from a grassroots voluntary initiative into a professional service delivery agency over the past 20 years. Our evolving HR policy has played a crucial role in this journey. We began by recruiting from local field areas and gradually transitioned to onboarding qualified professionals through academic collaborations, ensuring job guarantees to selected candidates. Currently, we hire around 20 professionals yearly—ranging from trainees to senior experts—and also collaborate with external agencies for specialized functions.
