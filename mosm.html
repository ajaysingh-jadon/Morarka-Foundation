MSOM Project – Meghalaya

The State Organic Mission is dedicated to promoting certified organic farming, strengthening value chains, and improving market access for farmers across the state. Our goal is to create sustainable livelihood opportunities while enhancing the production and visibility of high-value organic crops.

Home/Project / Mosm

What We Do

1

Develop Integrated Village Cooperative Society (IVCS) & Crop-Specific Clusters: We provide infrastructural, technical, and financial support to farmers, helping them form strong, organized groups and commodity-specific production clusters.

2

Facilitate Partnerships: We connect farmers with local enterprises and ICS, enabling long-term trade relations with domestic and international markets.

3

Empower Farmers: Farmers are organized into Farmer Interest Groups (FIGs) and gradually federated into IVCS, giving them full ownership of programs and initiatives.

4

Promote Sustainable Farming: We support the transition from conventional or subsistence farming to high-value, self-sustainable organic enterprises using local resources.

5

Training & Capacity-Building: Farmers receive continuous guidance on organic farming methods to improve productivity, quality, and market competitiveness.

6

Develop Value Chains & Organic Parks: We build integrated production, processing, storage, and marketing facilities, including organic parks and zones for high-investment crops.

7

Brand Building & Market Access: We help growers’ organizations develop branded organic products and strengthen marketing channels at all levels — local, national, and international.

8

State Lead Agency Support: Our dedicated agency coordinates, monitors, and finances initiatives to ensure smooth development and operationalization of the organic value chain.

Through these initiatives, the State Organic Mission is committed to creating a thriving organic ecosystem, empowering farmers, and connecting them to premium markets for a sustainable and prosperous future.
